About The Course

Primavera is a project management software that is used globally for planning, managing and controlling project charges, and their activities, with more efficiency and ease. The Software is widely used and more seen in the fields like Architecture, Engineering and Construction industry. Primavera includes other enterprise software such as Oracle and SAP’s ERP systems for better quality functioning and the software mainly focuses on project portfolio management and its solutions. Primavera's software pack includes P6, Pro sight, Contract Manager, Cost Manager, Pertmaster, SureTrak, Evolve and Inspire. The newest addition of primavera is Primavera P6, which is an integrated PPM solution that provides a real-time view of portfolio performance.
